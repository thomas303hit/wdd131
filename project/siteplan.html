FitLife Gym - Site Plan

Site Name

FitLife Gym

A modern, friendly name that communicates fitness and lifestyle improvement.

Optional domain: www.fitlifegym.com

Site Purpose

The website will provide membership plan details, class schedules, trainer profiles, and contact information to encourage new sign-ups and inform current members.

Scenarios

  • What are the membership prices and available plans?
  • Which classes are available this week and who are the trainers?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Blue (#1E90FF) → Used for titles and buttons.

Accent Color: Orange (#F4A261) → Used for call-to-action highlights.

Typography

Headings: Montserrat, sans-serif

Body: Roboto, sans-serif
